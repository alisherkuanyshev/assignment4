Who are we?

Our company, Coral Reefs Inc., is a pioneer in the field of ecological conservation and memorialization. We have recognized the devastating effects of climate change and rising ocean temperatures on coral reefs worldwide. To address this issue, we have developed a unique approach to preserving the memories of loved ones while supporting marine life and coral growth for centuries to come.

What does our project implement?

Our project revolves around creating eternal reefs by incorporating cremated remains into environmentally friendly cement mixtures. These mixtures are then used to construct artificial reefs, which are placed in the ocean at chosen locations. Our company has received approval from the Environmental Protection Agency, and our reefs are strategically located in fishing and diving areas.

On the last journey...

Families and friends can participate in the process by mixing the remains into the concrete and personalizing the memorial with handprints and written messages. The resulting "pearls" are then placed in a frame, accompanied by a plaque displaying the initials and life dates of the deceased. During a special ceremony, the reef is submerged in the ocean, becoming a permanent part of the marine ecosystem.

Why is it important?

Our company believes in the importance of coral reefs, not only for their beauty but also for their vital role in coastal protection and their therapeutic microorganism-rich environment. By combining memorialization with ecological preservation, we offer a meaningful and sustainable alternative to traditional burials.
